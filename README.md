Mental Health Companion (MHC)
1.Introduction
Mental health is an essential part of overall well-being, yet many people do not have easy access to tools that help them understand and manage their emotions.
The Mental Health Companion (MHC) is a digital solution designed to provide a simple, private, and supportive environment where users can reflect on their feelings and build healthier emotional habits.

The application focuses on three core pillars:

Self-expression (Journal)

Emotional awareness (Mood Tracking)

Supportive interaction (Chat AI)

By integrating these features into one platform, MHC encourages consistent mental wellness practices.

2.Problem Statement
Many individuals experience stress, anxiety, or emotional fluctuations but lack:

A safe place to express their thoughts

Awareness of their emotional patterns

Immediate emotional support

Traditional methods like therapy may not always be accessible due to time, cost, or social stigma.
Therefore, a digital companion can provide accessible and daily support.

3.Aim of the Project
To design and develop a user-friendly application that helps individuals:

Track their emotions

Reflect on daily experiences

Receive supportive interaction

Improve emotional awareness

4.Objectives
Provide a secure journaling platform

Enable easy mood logging

Offer supportive conversational interaction

Encourage daily mental wellness habits

Store data for personal reflection

5.Scope of the System
The system is intended for:

Students

Working professionals

Anyone interested in self-care

It is not a replacement for professional therapy but acts as a self-help and awareness tool.
